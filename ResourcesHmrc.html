HMRC Resources

VAT Notices

VAT Notices are HMRC's guidance intended for public use. Unless specified otherwise, notices do not have the force of law. However, they generally reflect HMRC's view of legislation.

The main document is Notice 700 - The VAT Guide.

Other notices are listed numerically and alphabetically.

VAT Manuals

VAT Manuals are HMRC's internal guidance documents intended for use by their staff. As with notices, unless specified otherwise, notices do not have the force of law. However, they generally reflect HMRC's view of legislation.

The majority of the manuals are publicly available under Open Government, although there are some redactions.
